INFUSION THERAPY PRIOR AUTHORIZATION

SUBMISSION METHODS:

ONLINE (RECOMMENDED): CignaforHCP Portal
- Log in at www.cignaforhcp.com
- Navigate to Precertification > New Request
- System availability: M-F 6am-11pm EST

FAX: (800) 768-4695
- Use form CG-PA-001
- Processing time: 7-10 business days

PHONE (URGENT/STATUS): (800) 768-4688
- M-F 8am-8pm EST, Sat 9am-1pm EST

REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION:
• Cigna Precertification Form (CG-PA-001)
• Physician notes within 60 days
• Diagnostic results
• Treatment plan
• Prior treatment history for biologics

IMPORTANT DRUG UPDATES FOR 2025:
- Remicade: Biosimilar preferred for new starts effective July 2025
- Tysabri: JCV antibody testing now required every 6 months (previously annual)
- Entyvio: Anti-TNF failure documentation now requires specific drug names and durations

TURNAROUND TIMES:
Standard: 7 business days
Urgent: 24 hours
Note: If submitting via fax, allow additional 2-3 business days for intake processing
