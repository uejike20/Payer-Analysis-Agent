CIGNA MEDICAL COVERAGE POLICY
Infusion Therapy Prior Authorization Requirements

EFFECTIVE DATE: July 1, 2024

1. HOW TO SUBMIT

Prior authorization for infused medications may be submitted through:

A. CignaforHCP Portal (www.cignaforhcp.com)
   - Select Precertification > New Request
   - Recommended for fastest processing

B. Fax: (800) 768-4695
   - Use Cigna Precertification Request Form (CG-PA-001)
   - Allow 7-10 business days for processing

C. Phone: (800) 768-4688
   - For urgent requests or status checks
   - M-F 8am-8pm EST, Sat 9am-1pm EST

2. DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

All requests must include:
- Cigna Precertification Request Form
- Treating physician's notes (within 60 days)
- Diagnostic test results supporting the diagnosis
- Treatment plan including dosing schedule
- Prior treatment history (required for all biologic medications)

3. DRUG-SPECIFIC POLICIES

REMICADE (infliximab):
- Requires documentation of conventional therapy failure
- Auth period: 6 months (initial), 12 months (renewal)
- Standard turnaround: 7 business days
- NOTE: Effective 2025, biosimilar preferred for new starts

ENTYVIO (vedolizumab):
- For Crohn's or UC only — diagnosis must be confirmed
- Requires failure of anti-TNF therapy OR documented contraindication
- Auth period: 6 months
- Standard turnaround: 7 business days

KEYTRUDA (pembrolizumab):
- FDA-approved indication documentation required
- Biomarker testing per NCCN guidelines
- Auth period: 3 months
- Standard turnaround: 5 business days

TYSABRI (natalizumab):
- JCV antibody testing REQUIRED before authorization
- TOUCH Prescribing Program enrollment verification required
- Auth period: 12 months
- Standard turnaround: 7 business days

4. TURNAROUND TIMES
Standard: 7 business days
Urgent: 24 hours (must specify urgent on form and call to confirm receipt)

5. APPEALS
Fax appeals to (800) 768-4699
Phone appeals: (800) 768-4688, Option 4
