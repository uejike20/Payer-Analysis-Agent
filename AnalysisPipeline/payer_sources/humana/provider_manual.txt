HUMANA PROVIDER MANUAL
Prior Authorization: Injectable & Infusible Drugs
2024 Guidelines

SECTION 1: HOW TO SUBMIT

Humana accepts prior authorization requests for infused medications via:

PREFERRED — Availity (www.availity.com)
Select Humana > Authorizations > New Auth Request
Turnaround: 5-7 business days

FAX — (800) 523-0023
Use Humana Prior Authorization Form (HUM-AUTH-2024)
Turnaround: 7-10 business days

PHONE — (800) 555-2546 (Provider PA Hotline)
For urgent/expedited requests only
M-F 8am-5pm CST
Must fax supporting documentation within 48 hours

STATUS CHECKS — (800) 555-2546, Option 2
Or check via Availity portal

SECTION 2: DOCUMENTATION

Required for all infusion drug PAs:
- Humana PA Form (HUM-AUTH-2024)
- Physician notes (within 60 days)
- Lab results relevant to diagnosis
- Letter of medical necessity
- Treatment plan with dosing schedule
- Insurance verification confirmation

SECTION 3: DRUG FORMULARY NOTES

REMICADE: Step therapy required — must trial conventional DMARD first. Biosimilar attestation required for new starts. Auth: 6 months.

RITUXAN: CD20+ documentation. Step therapy required for RA indication only. Auth: 6 months.

ENTYVIO: Anti-TNF failure or contraindication documentation. Auth: 6 months.

OCREVUS: MS specialist must submit. JCV testing recommended. Auth: 12 months.

SECTION 4: TURNAROUND
Standard: 7 business days
Urgent: 48 hours
