AETNA PROVIDER MANUAL — 2025 EDITION
Chapter 12: Prior Authorization Requirements for Infused Medications

12.1 SUBMISSION METHODS

Prior authorization requests for infused medications should be submitted via fax to:

    Aetna PA Department Fax: (888) 267-3277

Requests may also be submitted through the Availity portal at www.availity.com. Providers must have an active Availity account linked to their Aetna provider ID.

NOTE: Phone submissions are NOT accepted for infused medication PAs. Phone inquiries regarding PA status can be directed to (800) 624-0756, Option 3, then Option 1.

12.2 REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION

All PA requests for infused medications must include:
- Completed Aetna Prior Authorization Request Form (Form AET-PA-2025)
- Current chart notes (within 60 days)
- Lab results supporting medical necessity (within 90 days)
- Letter of medical necessity from prescribing physician
- For biologic medications: documentation of step therapy compliance (see Section 12.4)

12.3 DRUG-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

REMICADE (infliximab):
- Diagnosis must be confirmed by specialist
- Documentation of failure on conventional therapy required
- Initial auth: 6 months. Renewal: 12 months.
- Turnaround: 5 business days standard, 24 hours urgent

KEYTRUDA (pembrolizumab):
- PD-L1 testing results required
- Oncologist letter of medical necessity required
- Initial auth: 3 months. Renewal: 6 months.
- Turnaround: 5 business days standard, 24 hours urgent

RITUXAN (rituximab):
- CD20 positive status documentation required
- Initial auth: 6 months. Renewal: 12 months.
- Turnaround: 5 business days standard, 24 hours urgent

12.4 STEP THERAPY REQUIREMENTS

For Remicade and Entyvio: member must have documented trial and failure of at least one conventional therapy before biologic will be authorized. Documentation must include dates of therapy, duration, and reason for discontinuation.

12.5 TURNAROUND TIMES

Standard requests: 5 business days from receipt of complete submission
Urgent requests: 24 hours from receipt
Incomplete submissions will receive a request for additional information within 2 business days.
