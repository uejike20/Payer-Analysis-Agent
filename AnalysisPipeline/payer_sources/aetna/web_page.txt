PRIOR AUTHORIZATION FOR INFUSION MEDICATIONS

Effective January 1, 2024, all infusion medication prior authorization requests must be submitted through one of the following methods:

PREFERRED: Availity Portal (www.availity.com)
- Log in with your Aetna provider credentials
- Select "Authorizations & Referrals"
- Average turnaround: 3-5 business days

FAX: (888) 267-3277
- Use Aetna PA Request Form AET-PA-2024
- Average turnaround: 5-7 business days

PHONE: PA status inquiries only — (800) 624-0756, Option 3

REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION:
• Completed PA Request Form
• Chart notes (within 60 days of request)
• Supporting lab results
• Letter of medical necessity
• Step therapy documentation (where applicable)

DRUG-SPECIFIC NOTES:
Remicade: Requires step therapy documentation. Initial auth period: 6 months.
Keytruda: PD-L1 testing required. Initial auth period: 3 months.
Rituxan: CD20+ documentation required. Initial auth period: 6 months.
Entyvio: Requires step therapy documentation. Initial auth period: 6 months.

For questions, contact Aetna Provider Services at (800) 624-0756.
