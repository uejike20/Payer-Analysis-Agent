UNITEDHEALTHCARE PROVIDER MANUAL — 2025
Section 8: Prior Authorization for Specialty & Infused Medications

8.1 SUBMISSION METHODS

UnitedHealthcare accepts prior authorization requests through the following channels:

PRIMARY: UHC Provider Portal (www.uhcprovider.com)
- Navigate to Prior Auth > New Request
- Fastest turnaround: 3-5 business days

FAX: (800) 699-4711
- Attach UHC Prior Authorization Form UHC-PA-100
- Turnaround: 5-7 business days

PHONE: For urgent requests ONLY — (800) 331-6689
- Available M-F, 8am-6pm EST
- Must follow up with written documentation within 48 hours

8.2 REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION

- UHC Prior Authorization Form (UHC-PA-100)
- Clinical notes from most recent visit (within 60 days)
- Relevant lab/diagnostic results
- Prescribing physician's letter of medical necessity
- Prescription/order for the infused medication
- For biologics: prior treatment history

8.3 DRUG-SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS

REMICADE (infliximab):
- Must document failure of methotrexate or other conventional DMARD (RA indication)
- Weight-based dosing documentation required
- Auth period: 6 months initial, 12 months renewal
- Biosimilar preferred — must document medical rationale if requesting brand

KEYTRUDA (pembrolizumab):
- Biomarker testing results (PD-L1, MSI-H, TMB) as applicable
- Staging documentation
- Auth period: 3 months, renewable

OCREVUS (ocrelizumab):
- MS diagnosis confirmed by neurologist
- MRI results within 12 months
- Auth period: 12 months

8.4 IMPORTANT NOTES

- Submissions missing required documentation will be pended for 14 calendar days
- If documentation not received within 14 days, request is auto-denied
- All PA determinations are made within 5 business days of complete submission
- Urgent requests: determination within 24 hours
