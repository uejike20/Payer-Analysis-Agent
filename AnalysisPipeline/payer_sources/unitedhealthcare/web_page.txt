PRIOR AUTHORIZATION: INFUSION & SPECIALTY DRUGS

Effective March 1, 2026, UnitedHealthcare has updated the prior authorization process for infusion medications.

SUBMISSION OPTIONS:

1. UHC PROVIDER PORTAL (PREFERRED)
   www.uhcprovider.com > Prior Auth > Specialty Drug Request
   Use form UHC-PA-200 (Specialty Drug Prior Authorization)
   Turnaround: 3-5 business days

2. FAX
   Specialty Infusion PA Fax: (800) 699-4702
   General Medical PA Fax: (800) 699-4711
   Turnaround: 5-7 business days
   NOTE: Use (800) 699-4702 for faster processing of infusion drugs

3. PHONE (URGENT ONLY)
   (800) 331-6689 — M-F, 8am-6pm EST
   Written follow-up required within 48 hours

WHAT'S NEW (March 2026):
• Biosimilar step therapy is now REQUIRED for Remicade, Rituxan, and Herceptin
• New form UHC-PA-200 replaces UHC-PA-100 for specialty drugs
• Chart note window extended to 90 days (previously 60 days)
• Portal submissions now receive auto-acknowledgment within 1 business day

REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION:
• Form UHC-PA-200
• Chart notes (within 90 days — updated March 2026)
• Lab results
• Letter of medical necessity
• Step therapy/biosimilar documentation (for applicable drugs)

DRUG QUICK REFERENCE:
| Drug | Step Therapy Required | Auth Period | Special Requirements |
|------|----------------------|-------------|---------------------|
| Remicade | Yes - biosimilar first | 6 months | Weight-based dosing docs |
| Keytruda | No | 3 months | Biomarker testing |
| Rituxan | Yes - biosimilar first | 6 months | CD20+ documentation |
| Ocrevus | No | 12 months | Neurologist confirmation |
| Herceptin | Yes - biosimilar first | 6 months | HER2 testing |

Questions? Call (800) 331-6689 or message through the portal.
