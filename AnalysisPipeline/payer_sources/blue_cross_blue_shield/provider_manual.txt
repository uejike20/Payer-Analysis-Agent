ANTHEM BLUE CROSS BLUE SHIELD
Provider Reference Guide 2025
Medical Drug Prior Authorization

NOTE: BCBS plans vary by state. This guide covers Anthem BCBS commercial plans in CA, NY, OH, GA, IN, and VA. For other Blue plans, contact the local plan directly.

SUBMISSION METHODS

1. ONLINE: Provider portal at www.anthem.com/providers
   - Select Medical Drug Auth > New Submission
   - Turnaround: 3-5 business days

2. FAX: (844) 405-0296
   - Use Anthem Medical Drug PA Form (ANT-MED-PA-25)
   - Turnaround: 5-7 business days

3. PHONE: (800) 274-7767
   - For urgent authorization requests only
   - Follow up with written documentation within 24 hours
   - M-F 8am-6pm local time

DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

Standard documentation for all infused drug PAs:
- Completed PA form (ANT-MED-PA-25)
- Office visit notes (within 60 days)
- Relevant labs/diagnostics
- Letter of medical necessity
- Current medication list

DRUG-SPECIFIC NOTES:

REMICADE: Step therapy required (biosimilar first for new starts). Prior DMARD trial required for RA indication.
KEYTRUDA: Biomarker documentation. Oncology treatment plan required.
OCREVUS: Neurologist must submit. MRI within 12 months.
HERCEPTIN: HER2 testing results. Biosimilar preferred for new starts.

TURNAROUND:
Standard: 5 business days
Urgent: 24 hours

APPEALS:
Fax: (844) 405-0299
Phone: (800) 274-7767, Option 5
